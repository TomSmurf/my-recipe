MyRecipe Privacy Policy

We believe you should always know what data we collect from you and how we use it, and that you should have meaningful control over both. We want to empower you to make the best decisions about the information that you share with us.

MyRecipe is public and anyone around the world can join us. We give you non-public, protected ways to add your own recipes. You can use MyRecipe under a login if you prefer not to use your name.

When you use MyRecipe, even if you're not adding any recipes, we receive no personal information. You can choose to share additional information with us like your email address, first name and last name.

We give you control through your settings to limit the data we collect from you.

If you have questions about this policy, how we collect or process your personal data, or anything else related to our privacy practices, we want to hear from you. You can contact us at any time.
